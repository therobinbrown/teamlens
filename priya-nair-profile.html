Hours & timezone

10–6 IST (UTC+5:30). Mornings 10–12 are my focus time for design work; I'm most reachable in my afternoon, which overlaps with the team's morning.

How I communicate

A mix — quick things in chat, but for design decisions I like to write them up so the thinking is visible. Same-day reply is my norm.

Feedback that works for me

Scheduled and private, please, rather than a surprise in a group setting. Give me the context and I'll run with it.

In meetings

I warm up more in smaller groups. In big meetings I contribute better when there's a pre-read and I've had time to form a view.

Growing toward

Building my product-strategy muscle so I can shape the 'what' and 'why,' not just the 'how.'

Good to know

I'm on the far side of the timezone spread from most of the team, so I rely on clear written handoffs to stay in sync. I observe a few regional holidays the team calendar may not show.

What helps me feel supported

Being brought into decisions early — my best contribution comes when I'm in the room while things are still open, not after.
