How to work with me

The unspoken stuff.

Every team runs on things people rarely say out loud — when you focus best, how you like feedback, what drains you, what helps you feel included. This quick conversation gathers yours, so your teammates can work with you more thoughtfully from day one.

The point of this: awareness and empathy, not labels or evaluation. Your answers become a short "how to work with me" profile for the team — there are no wrong answers and nothing here is a test. Anything you'd rather not answer is optional; skip freely.

You decide who sees each answer. Every question has a "🔒 Who sees this?" control, set to Team by default — change any answer to whatever feels right:
  • Team — shown on your profile and visible to everyone.
  • Manager — shared with your manager, not the whole team.
  • AI-only — kept off your profile, but the collaboration assistant may use it to guide people without revealing the detail itself (for example, suggesting a better time to reach you without saying why).
  • Private — kept entirely to yourself and never used.
You'll review everything — and can adjust any privacy setting — before your profile is saved.
