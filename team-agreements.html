How we work together

Our working agreements.

The shared norms that let a distributed team move fast without everyone being online at once. These aren't rules handed down — they're agreements we keep, revisit, and reshape together.

Last updated: June 2026
01

Async first

Default to writing it down so anyone can catch up.

02

Clarity over speed

A clear message beats a fast, confusing one.

03

Trust by default

Assume good intent; read each other generously.

04

Work in the open

Share drafts early; visibility beats surprises.

These agreements are living.

If something here stops serving us, say so. We revisit these together — bring it up in a retro, a team sync, or just start a thread. The goal is a team that works well for everyone on it, not a rulebook nobody questions.
