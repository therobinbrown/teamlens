For managers

The team, at a glance.

Patterns across the team that are hard to see one profile at a time — when everyone overlaps, how people like feedback, who needs space to contribute, and where the team's strengths cluster. The goal is to lead with awareness, not to rank anyone.

🔒 Manager-only view. In a real deployment, this dashboard would be restricted to managers and team leads — it surfaces aggregated patterns (including manager-tier preferences) that aren't shown to the wider team. It's here in the demo so you can see the full picture.
This view is powered by the team data below — seeded with examples you can edit. In a hosted version with saved profiles, it would populate automatically from each person's intro.
