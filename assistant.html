How privacy works

Every piece of a profile carries a visibility level. The assistant respects them — it can even let private information shape a recommendation without revealing the reason behind it.
TeamVisible to everyone, and freely used in recommendations.
ManagerShown only to the person's manager (use the "Viewing as" toggle). For teammates, it stays hidden and only shapes advice.
AI-onlyThe assistant may use it to shape guidance without disclosing the detail itself.
PrivateNever shown and never used in recommendations.

Select a teammate to see how their profile is shared. Fields marked AI-only or Private are handled discreetly.
